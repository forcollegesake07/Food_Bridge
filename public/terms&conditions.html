Legal Center

Transparency is our priority.

1 Platform Role & Liability

FoodBridge acts solely as an intermediary technology platform connecting food donors (Restaurants) with recipients (Orphanages). We do not cook, handle, package, or transport any food items.

Disclaimer of Liability

FoodBridge, its developers, and affiliates are not responsible or liable for any health issues, food poisoning, allergic reactions, illness, or damages arising from the consumption of donated food. The responsibility for food safety lies entirely with the donor (Restaurant).

2 Strict Action Against Negligence

While FoodBridge claims no liability, we enforce a Zero Tolerance Policy regarding food safety to protect our community.

  • Reporting Mechanism: Orphanages are encouraged to report any instance of spoiled, stale, or unhygienic food immediately via the dashboard using the "Report Issue" button.
  • Administrative Action: Upon receiving a report, our Admin team will investigate. If a Restaurant is found to have donated unsafe food:
    • 1st Offense: Formal Warning & 7-day account suspension.
    • Repeated Offense: Permanent ban from the platform and potential reporting to local food safety authorities.

3 User Obligations

For Restaurants (Donors):

  • You represent and warrant that all food donated is fresh, edible, and packaged hygienically in food-grade containers.
  • You must provide an accurate location pin (within 15km accuracy) to ensure feasible pickups.
  • No Leftovers: You must not donate food that has been served to customers or touched. Only untouched surplus food from the kitchen is permitted.
  • Accurate Imagery: Photos uploaded must represent the actual food being donated. Misleading stock photos are prohibited.

For Orphanages (Recipients):

  • You agree to arrange transportation and pick up claimed donations within the agreed timeframe.
  • You must inspect food upon collection. Accepting the food implies you are satisfied with its visible condition and temperature.
  • False reports against restaurants for personal grievances will result in immediate account suspension.

4 Account Management & Verification

FoodBridge reserves the right to manage user access to maintain platform integrity.

  • Verification: Accounts may be marked as "Verified" (Blue Check) by Admins after successful document verification or history of successful donations. This status can be revoked at any time.
  • Suspension: Admins retain the right to disable any account without prior notice for violation of terms, suspicious activity, or inactivity.
  • Broadcasts: By using the platform, you agree to receive system-wide broadcasts and urgent notifications from the Administrator regarding service status or emergency appeals.

5 Logistics & Indemnification

FoodBridge does not provide logistics services. The arrangement of transport is the sole responsibility of the recipient (Orphanage) unless the Restaurant voluntarily offers delivery.

You agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless FoodBridge, its creators, and affiliates from any claims, liabilities, damages, or legal fees arising out of your use of the platform.

6 Content & Disputes

  • Image Uploads: You retain ownership of images uploaded, but grant FoodBridge a license to display them on the platform. You must not upload copyrighted or offensive material.
  • Dispute Resolution: In case of a dispute between a Donor and Recipient, the FoodBridge Admin acts as the final arbitrator. The Admin's decision regarding account suspension or warning points is final.

Information We Collect

To operate FoodBridge effectively, we collect the following types of information when you register:

  • Identity Data: Organization Name, Contact Person Name.
  • Contact Data: Email address, Phone number.
  • Technical Data: Login credentials (via Google Auth or Email/Password), IP address, and Device type.
  • Geolocation Data: Precise latitude and longitude coordinates when you use the "Pin Location" feature.
  • Content Data: Images of food uploaded to the platform.

Third-Party Service Providers

We share specific data with trusted third-party services to provide core functionalities:

  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue): Your name, email address, and order details are transmitted to Brevo API to send transactional emails (e.g., "Donation Claimed", "Receipt Confirmed"). By using this service, you consent to receiving these automated system emails.
  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication, database storage, and hosting. Google may store cookies to maintain your login session.
  • OpenStreetMap & Nominatim: Your latitude/longitude coordinates are sent to these services to generate human-readable address data and map visualizations.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party providers, though we select them based on their reliability and security standards.

How We Use Location Data

Your location data is critical to the core functionality of FoodBridge. We use your coordinates specifically to:

  • Calculate the distance between Restaurants and Orphanages.
  • Filter "Urgent Requests" and "Available Donations" to show only those within a 10-15km operational radius.
  • Provide accurate pickup addresses to the other party once a donation is claimed.

We do not track your location in real-time background services; we only store the static location you explicitly save in your profile.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy. We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

However, essential contact information (Phone, Address, Name) is shared automatically with:

  • The Counterparty: When a donation is claimed, the Restaurant sees the Orphanage's details, and vice versa, to facilitate the pickup.
  • Service Providers: As mentioned above (Brevo, Firebase).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information and transaction history for as long as your account is active to provide you with history logs and statistics. If you wish to delete your account, please contact the Administrator. Note that some transaction records may be anonymized and kept for platform analytics.

By continuing to use FoodBridge, you acknowledge that you have read and understood these policies.
